Movie Fights Overview

Disclaimer: This site is an unofficial fan effort, every effort has been made to ensure accuracy

Judge

Andy Signore

Fact Checker

Dan Murrell

Participants

  • 1 Judge (usually Andy)
  • 1 Fact Checker (Usually Dan)
  • 3 "Fighters"

Structure:

5 preliminary rounds (plus Tiebreakers if needed)
- Answers are prepared in advance
- Answers are shared among fighters to prepare counterarguments
- If 2 fighters pick the same answer a coin flip decides who gets to use it
5 speed rounds (plus Tiebreaker if needed)
- Top 2 Fighters compete head to head
- Answers are not prepared
- Each fighter gets 20sec. + a 10sec. rebuttal to argue their case
- Whoever answers first argues first
- If both pick the same answer whoever said it first gets to use it

Judging Criteria:

  • Quality of argument made
  • Passion
  • Creativity

The show usually airs live every Thursday at 4pm PST on SJPlus viewers can play along on twitter at #MovieFightsLive and each round Dan will read out some interesting answers, poll results, and good comments
